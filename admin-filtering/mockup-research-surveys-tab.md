# UI/UX Wireframe & Spec: Research Surveys Tab

## 1. Visual Layout
**Style:** Dark Mode interface. Textless logo in the header.

### Top Section: Survey Data Ingestion
* **Upload Zone:** "Upload Google Forms CSV Export"
* **Sync Button:** [ Sync Survey Data with Chat Logs ]

### Bottom Section: Qualitative Dashboard
* **Left Panel: Helpfulness Scores**
  * Bar chart mapping the 1-5 helpfulness scale (from the uploaded survey PDF) against the total number of respondents.
* **Right Panel: Cross-Referenced Feedback**
  * Displays specific text answers from the survey (e.g., "What part of the explanation made it transferable?") directly alongside the student's corresponding anonymized chat history from that same week.
* **Export Action:** [ Generate Combined Research CSV ]
